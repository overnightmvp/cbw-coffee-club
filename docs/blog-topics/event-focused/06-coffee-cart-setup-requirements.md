### Space Requirements (400 words)

**Minimum Footprint**
- Standard: 2m x 2m (most carts)
- Compact carts: 1.5m x 1.5m (rare)
- Luxury setups: 2.5m x 3m (with extras)

**Clearance Zones**
- Queue space: 2m in front
- Barista workspace: 1m behind (some carts)
- Side access: 0.5m each side (restocking)
- Total recommended: 4m x 3m area

**Floor Surface**
- Level, firm surface required
- Grass: Need plywood base
- Gravel: Challenging, not ideal
- Uneven surfaces: Adjustable feet (ask vendor)

**Indoor Considerations**
- Doorway width: Minimum 90cm
- Elevator access: Check dimensions
- Stairs: Most carts cannot navigate
- Ceiling height: Minimum 2.5m (steam clearance)

### Power Requirements (500 words)

**Standard Setup**
- 2x 10-amp outlets (standard Australian GPO)
- Must be within 10m of cart location
- Separate circuits preferred (avoid tripping)
- Total draw: ~2400W typical

**Extension Cords**
- Vendor usually provides
- Must be commercial-grade
- Taped down for safety
- Length limitations: Prefer <20m

**Generator Option**
- For outdoor venues without power
- Vendor may provide (additional cost $100-200)
- Noise consideration (briefing)
- Fuel capacity for event duration

**Power Failure Contingency**
- Backup plan with venue
- Battery-powered grinder option (rare)
- Communicate risks for outdoor events

**What Draws Power**
- Espresso machine: 1800-2200W (main load)
- Grinder: 300-500W
- Milk fridge: 100-150W
- Lighting (if evening): 50-100W

### Water Requirements (300 words)

**Water Supply**
- Most carts have onboard tank (90L typical)
- Sufficient for 180-200 cups
- Larger events: May need refill access
- Plumbed connection: Rare, ask vendor

**Wastewater**
- Onboard tank (36L typical)
- Vendor manages disposal
- Venue: Designate disposal location
- No sink access needed (usually)

**Backup Water**
- Vendor brings extra if needed
- Large events: Discuss capacity upfront

### Access & Load-In Requirements (400 words)

**Vehicle Access**
- Carts transported in vans/trucks
- Need parking within 50m (ideal)
- Loading dock vs ground level
- Freight elevator (if upper floors)

**Setup Timeline**
- Arrival: 45-60 min before service
- Setup: 30-40 minutes
- Testing: 5-10 minutes
- Pack-down: 30-40 minutes
- Total venue time: 2-3 hours beyond service

**Access Restrictions**
- Discuss with venue coordinator
- Loading zone time limits
- Parking permit requirements (CBD)
- Building security protocols

**Vendor Delivery**
- Most vendors deliver and setup
- "Drop-off only" options (rare, cheaper)
- Confirm in quote

### Environmental Factors (300 words)

**Weather Protection**
- Rain: Need covered area or marquee
- Wind: Secure setup, avoid >30km/h
- Heat: Direct sun affects machine temp
- Cold: Steam production may slow

**Outdoor Recommendations**
- Shade or marquee strongly recommended
- Windbreak if breezy location
- Ground protection (tarp if grass)

### Common Setup Issues & Solutions (350 words)

**Issue: No nearby power**
- Solution: Generator rental, relocate cart, extension planning

**Issue: Uneven ground**
- Solution: Plywood base, adjustable feet, relocate

**Issue: Narrow doorway**
- Solution: Disassemble cart (some models), use smaller cart

**Issue: Venue restrictions on arrival time**
- Solution: Early access negotiation, day-before setup (rare)

**Issue: Weather concerns (outdoor)**
- Solution: Marquee rental, backup indoor location
