## How to Choose Coffee Service for Your Glen Iris Event

Glen Iris events typically prioritize quality over quantity, with smaller guest counts and higher expectations for presentation and specialty coffee. Consider these factors:

**For intimate weddings (40-80 guests):** Bean & Cart Co. and Java Junction offer the personalized service and aesthetic that suit garden weddings and heritage venues.

**For corporate events:** Espresso Express and Percolate deliver the professionalism and coffee expertise expected in Glen Iris's business community.

**For coffee enthusiasts:** Percolate's roastery background and educational approach creates memorable experiences for guests who appreciate coffee culture.

**Venue considerations:** Many Glen Iris events occur at private residences or parks like Glen Iris Park. Confirm vendors have experience with residential street access and can manage council permits for public spaces.

**Questions to ask:**

1. Have you worked at residential Glen Iris venues before?
2. Do you handle council permits for park events?
3. Can you customize service for smaller, intimate gatherings?
4. What's your approach to quiet, elegant service (versus high-volume efficiency)?

## Getting Around for Your Event

**Venue access:** Residential events require clear communication about street parking for vendor vehicles and equipment access to backyards/gardens.

**Public spaces:** Glen Iris Park events may require City of Boroondara permits (apply 2-4 weeks ahead).

**Vendor logistics:** Most coffee carts serve Glen Iris from Chadstone, Malvern, or Oakleigh bases (5-10km radius).

**Guest arrivals:** Tram routes 6, 5, and 8 provide public transport, but most guests arrive by car. Residential events should communicate parking arrangements clearly.
