## Wedding Coffee Cart Timing & Service

### When to Schedule Coffee Service

**Ceremony Arrival Coffee:**
- Guest refreshment before ceremony begins
- 30-60 minutes pre-ceremony
- Creates welcoming atmosphere

**Cocktail Hour Coffee:**
- During photos between ceremony and reception
- 60-90 minutes during golden hour
- Keeps guests refreshed and entertained

**Reception Coffee:**
- After dinner, with dessert
- 1-3 hours late reception
- Provides alternative to alcohol

**Late-Night Coffee:**
- Final hour of reception
- Sobers up guests for safe driving
- Pairs with late-night snacks

### Recommended Service Duration

**Minimum:** 2 hours (covers cocktail hour or reception coffee)

**Typical:** 3-4 hours (cocktail hour + reception service)

**Extended:** 5-6 hours (arrival + cocktail + reception)

Most couples book 3-4 hours covering cocktail hour and early reception.

## Wedding Venue Logistics

### Power Requirements

Most coffee carts need:
- Standard 240V power outlet (10-15 amp)
- Power within 20 meters of cart position
- Backup generator if outdoor venue lacks power

Confirm power availability with venue before booking cart.

### Cart Positioning

**Best Locations:**
- Near ceremony exit (arrival coffee)
- Cocktail area edge (doesn't block flow)
- Reception entrance (late-night service)

**Avoid:**
- Blocking guest movement
- Far from power source
- Direct sun for extended periods
- Uneven ground

### Venue Coordination

Inform coffee cart vendor about:
- Venue address and parking/access
- Setup timing (usually 1 hour before service)
- Power source locations
- Any venue restrictions or requirements
- Wedding timeline and key moments

## Wedding Coffee Cart FAQs

**Do we need to provide anything?**
No—carts bring everything (beans, milk, cups, equipment).

**Can they create signature drinks?**
Most vendors will create 1-2 custom drinks with advance notice.

**What about dietary restrictions?**
Multiple alternative milks standard (oat, almond, soy).

**How long does setup take?**
Usually 30-60 minutes before service starts.

**Can they serve during ceremony?**
Some couples offer pre-ceremony coffee; carts usually setup during ceremony.

**What if it rains?**
Carts need covered area—coordinate marquee or indoor backup.
