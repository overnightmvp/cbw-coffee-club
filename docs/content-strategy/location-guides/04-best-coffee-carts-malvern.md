## Getting Around for Your Event

**Venue access:** Premium Malvern venues typically have established caterer protocols. Confirm loading access, timing windows, and any venue-preferred vendor lists.

**Residential events:** Many Malvern celebrations occur at private homes. Coordinate street parking for vendor vehicles and access to setup locations.

**Public spaces:** Council permits from City of Stonnington required for park events (apply 2-4 weeks ahead).

**Vendor logistics:** Most coffee carts serve Malvern from local bases within 5-10km radius (Chadstone, Glen Iris, Armadale).

**Guest arrivals:** Tram routes and Malvern station provide public transport, but most guests arrive by car. Premium venues typically have parking or valet services.
