    Write 10 venue spotlight posts (800-1200 words each) using validated venue data from `chadstone-venues.md`.

    **Post template structure:**

    ```markdown
    # [Business Name]: [Specialty Coffee/Service] in [Suburb]

    ## Introduction (100-150 words)
    - What makes this vendor unique
    - Key specialty or differentiator
    - Who they serve (weddings, corporate, private events)

    ## About [Business Name] (200-300 words)
    - Business story and background
    - Specialty coffee offerings
    - Event types they cater to
    - Coverage area (for mobile carts) or location (for coffee shops)

    ## Menu & Offerings (150-200 words)
    - Coffee types (espresso, filter, cold brew)
    - Specialty drinks
    - Add-ons (pastries, snacks)
    - Equipment quality (espresso machines, grinders)

    ## Pricing & Capacity (100-150 words)
    - Pricing model (per hour, per cup, package deals)
    - Guest capacity
    - Minimum booking requirements

    ## Why Choose [Business Name] for Your Event (150-200 words)
    - Event suitability (weddings, corporate, markets)
    - Barista quality
    - Customer reviews or testimonials (if available)
    - Unique value proposition

    ## How to Book (100-150 words)
    - Booking process
    - Lead time requirements
    - Link to vendor profile on The Bean Route
    - Call-to-action: "Inquire Now" or "Get a Quote"

    ## Conclusion (50-100 words)
    - Recap of key benefits
    - Invitation to explore other Chadstone coffee options
    ```

    **SEO optimization per post:**
    - **Title:** "[Business Name]: [Specialty] in [Suburb] | The Bean Route"
    - **Excerpt:** 150-160 characters describing the vendor and event suitability
    - **Target Keywords:**
      - Primary: "[business name] coffee cart" or "[business name] [suburb]"
      - Secondary: "hire coffee cart [suburb]", "mobile barista [suburb]", "specialty coffee [suburb]"
    - **Internal Links (from linking matrix):**
      - 2-3 location guides (e.g., "Best Coffee Carts in Chadstone")
      - 1-2 how-to guides (e.g., "How to Hire a Coffee Cart for Your Event")
      - 1 coffee education post (e.g., "What Makes a Great Barista")

    **Content principles:**
    - Authentic and informative (not salesy)
    - Australian English spelling and Melbourne-specific references
    - Pricing in AUD (if available)
    - Event organizer perspective (benefits to hiring this vendor)
    - Include actionable next steps (book, inquire, get quote)

    **Image placeholders:**
    - Document required images for each post (vendor logo, coffee cart photo, latte art, event setup)
    - Note: "PLACEHOLDER: Vendor storefront photo" if image not available
    - Source from vendor Instagram/Facebook or use stock coffee images temporarily

    **Why markdown drafts first:**
    - Easier batch editing and human review
    - Version control before Payload entry
    - AI-assisted drafting with human refinement workflow
    - Can commit drafts to git for collaboration

         - Headings (H2, H3)
         - Paragraphs
         - Bold/italic formatting
         - Internal links (use Payload link format, not markdown)
         - External links (vendor profile links: `/vendors/[slug]`)
       - **featuredImage:** Upload vendor photo or use placeholder (document if placeholder)
       - **status:** 'published'
       - **publishedAt:** Set to current date/time (stagger by 1 day for publishing cadence)

    4. **Category & Conversion:**
       - **category:** 'event-focused' (all spotlights are event-focused)
       - **conversionGoal:** 'vendor_signup' (spotlights drive vendor discovery â†’ inquiries)

    5. **SEO Fields:**
       - **targetKeywords:** Array of keywords from markdown frontmatter
         - Primary: "[business name] [suburb]"
         - Secondary: "hire coffee cart [suburb]", "mobile barista [suburb]"
       - **searchIntent:** "Find and book [business name] for events in [suburb]"
       - **metaDescription:** Same as excerpt (or custom if excerpt is too short)
       - **ogImage:** Same as featuredImage (or custom social media image)

    6. **Editorial Metadata:**
       - **priority:** 'quick-win' (venue spotlights are low-difficulty, fast ranking)
